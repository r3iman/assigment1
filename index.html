Americano

A classic espresso-based drink made with a single or double shot of espresso and hot water. The result is a strong yet smooth coffee that's perfect for those who prefer a more subtle taste than traditional drip coffee. Served hot in a mug or cup.

600 tenge

Cappuccino

A delicious and creamy espresso-based drink made with equal parts of espresso, steamed milk, and frothed milk. The layers of espresso, steamed milk, and frothed milk create a beautifully layered drink that's both rich and flavorful. Often served with a dusting of cocoa powder or cinnamon on top for added flavor and presentation. Served hot in a cup.

900 tenge

Ice Latte

Iced latte is a refreshing and delicious coffee drink that is perfect for hot weather. It is a simple yet flavorful drink made by combining espresso with cold milk and ice. The smooth and creamy texture of the milk combined with the bold taste of espresso creates a delightful balance of flavors.

1200 tenge
